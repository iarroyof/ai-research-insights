### 🏛️ About

**Sabia** is an AI-powered biomedical knowledge discovery platform designed for researchers, clinicians, and bioinformaticians. It enables semantic search across scientific literature, automatic extraction of biomedical relationships (subject-predicate-object triplets), and interactive knowledge graph visualization — transforming unstructured research into actionable insights.

🔬 **Version 1.0** focuses on **Lung Cancer** research, integrating thousands of knowledge triplets from peer-reviewed publications to accelerate hypothesis generation, literature review, and evidence synthesis.

*Empowering precision medicine through intelligent knowledge mining.*
